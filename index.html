GrandCanals
Inc.

June-July 2019

GrandCanals is a shipping analytics company I worked at during the summer before my senior year of high school. I worked on a single project and saw it through to completion during the five weeks I spent there. I was tasked with writing a front-end interface for a page of their web app with the design having been provided by a UX designer. The interface allowed users to enter information about a shipment such as size, weight, departure state, destination state, and departure date. With this form submitted, the user would then be shown the price of the shipment and the number of days needed to ship from point A to point B for each of the company's shipping priority options ("standard", "expedited", "premium"). A graphic would also be displayed to the user displaying a map of the United States with a line connecting thier desired departure and destination states. The interface was written in HTML, CSS with Bootstrap 4, and JavaScript with JQuery. This was my first exposure to both Bootstrap and JQuery, making this experience a great growth opportunity for my front-end development skills.
